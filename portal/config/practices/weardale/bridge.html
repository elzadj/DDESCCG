The Bridge Project for Young Carers in Durham aims to support young people who have challenging caring roles with a family focused approach. The Bridge approaches this through individual work, identified group work and working with others so that there is ‘no wrong door’ for a young carer in County Durham. The aim is to enable young carers to aspire to and achieve their full potential.

The Bridge Project offers:

  • A designated link worker
  • Information, advice and support
  • A comprehensive assessment
  • Individual support for Young Carers
  • Help with accessing services or breaks
  • Targeted group work
  • Support with school related issues
  • Opportunities to get involved in designing the services for Young Carers
